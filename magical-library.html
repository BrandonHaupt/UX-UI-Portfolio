About the project

ux/ui Case study

Campery is your go to app for planning your camping trip. It allows customers to pick dates on a calendar and plan their camping trip, it also has a guide to surviving in the wilderness and a store that allows them to purchase camping gear.

UX/UI Designer

02
Preliminaries

Problem Statement

Camping is fun as well as hard to do, you sometimes forget things at home, it’s a human thing to do. The challenge is to design an app that allows for reminding the users to not forget things, a system to help in case something happens, and a shopping place so you can easily go and buy the things you need and have it shipped right to your door.

Goals

My goal is to create an app that allows users to get reminders that their trip is coming up, to suggest that they bring a heavier blanket or camping bag because it’s going to be cold that week and if they don't have it to conveniently go to the online store and purchase one and have it shipped right to your doorstep.
