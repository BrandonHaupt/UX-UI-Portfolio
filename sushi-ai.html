About the project

Sushi Ai is a sushi restaurant has a unique feature, all you can eat sushi. For a decent price you can eat all the sushi, appetizer, and more.

My Role

UX/UI Designer

02
Preliminaries

Problem Statement

Sushi Ai has an outdated website design, no color theme, and no real way to order from their website, it takes you to a separate place to order. The site needs a better UI, color theme, and a responsive layout.

Goals

My goal is to change the site to a more modern and sleek design. Allow for responsive design and to order food from the site that will automatically go to their system.
